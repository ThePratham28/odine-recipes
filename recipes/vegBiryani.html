Vegetable Biryani

Description

Fragrant rice cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice, soaked
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 tablespoon biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-5 whole spices (cinnamon, cardamom, cloves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Sauté onions until golden brown in ghee.
  2. Add cumin seeds, whole spices, and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Layer soaked rice over vegetables, add water, cover, and cook until rice is done.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
