Aloo Gobi

Description

A simple and delicious dish featuring potatoes (aloo) and cauliflower (gobi).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, turmeric, coriander powder, and cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add potatoes and cauliflower, cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot.
