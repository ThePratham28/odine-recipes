Palak Paneer

Description

A nutritious dish featuring spinach and Indian cottage cheese (paneer).

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 4 cups spinach, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions until golden brown, add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add pureed tomatoes, cumin powder, garam masala, and cook until oil separates.
  3. Add chopped spinach, cook until wilted.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cream, simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Season with salt, serve hot with rice or naan.
