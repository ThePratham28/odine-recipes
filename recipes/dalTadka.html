Dal Tadka

Description

A simple and comforting lentil dish with a tempered mustard and cumin seed topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (dal)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook lentils with turmeric until soft.
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown in ghee, add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add this mixture to the cooked lentils.
  5. In a separate pan, heat ghee, add cumin and mustard seeds until they splutter.
  6. Pour the tempering over the lentils, garnish with fresh coriander.
