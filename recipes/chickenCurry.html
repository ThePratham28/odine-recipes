Chicken Curry

Description

A classic Indian dish with tender chicken pieces in a flavorful curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add chicken, cook until browned.
  5. Add water, cover, and simmer until chicken is cooked.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or naan.
